Prep: 15 minutes

Cook: 1 hour 30 minutes

Total: 1 hour 45 minutes

Ingredients

Meatloaf

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon poultry seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 medium brown onion (sauted)
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 3/4 cup Italian breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon celery salt
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1/2 cup milk

Sauce

  • 1/3 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon mustard

Mom's Meatloaf

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Saute the onion. Mix all the meatloaf ingredients in a tall pot or very large bowl.
  3. Cook in a 9x13 pan (do not grease pan) for 1 1/2 hours.
  4. While you're waiting for that, make the sauce with all the sauce ingredients.
  5. When there are 10 minutes left in the cooking (after 1 hour 20 minutes), spread the sauce on the meatloaf. Keep baking.
  6. Remove from the oven. Serve.
