Prep: 15 minutes

Cook: 25-30 hour

Cool: 2+ hours

Total: 2 hours 45 minutes

Less if you're impatient ;)

Ingredients

  • 14 oz light caramel
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups oats
  • 1 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups chocolate chips (or to taste)

Carmelitas

  1. Preheat oven for 350 degrees.
  2. Start melting the caramel and milk in a sauce pan on low heat. Don't let it burn.
  3. Mix the dry ingredients (flour, baking soda, salt, oats, brown sugar).
  4. Melt the butter and mix with the dry ingredients.
  5. Press 1/2 of the mixture into a 9x13 pan.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes.
  7. Remove from the oven. Spread the caramel mixture over the top. Sprinkle the chocolate chips over that.
  8. Bake for another 15-20 minutes.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for 2 hours.
