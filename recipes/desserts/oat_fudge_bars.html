Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3 cups oats
  • 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup butter (used later)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Oat Fudge Bars

  1. Preheat oven for 350 degrees.
  2. Cream the
    butter 1 cup
    , sugar, and eggs.
  3. Mix the flour, baking soda, and oats. Add the dry mixture slowly to the creamed mixture.
  4. Spread 3/4 of this into a 9x13 pan.
  5. Melt the remaining ingredients in a saucepan and pour over the dough in the pan.
  6. Blob the remaining oat mixture on top.
  7. Bake in the oven for 25 minutes.
