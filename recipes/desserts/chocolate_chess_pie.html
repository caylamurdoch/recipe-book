Prep: 15 minutes

Cook: 45 hour

Cool: ~ 1 hour

Total: 2 hours

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 can (5 oz) evaporated milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 9-inch uncooked pie shell

Auntie's Chocolate Chess Pie

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Mix the cocoa, flour, and sugar. Cream together with butter.
  3. Add milk, eggs, and vanilla. Beat until well mixed.
  4. Pour into pie shell (careful, the filling is generous).
  5. Bake for 45 minutes or until an inserted toothpick or pin comes out clean.
  6. Serve after it cools to room temperature.
