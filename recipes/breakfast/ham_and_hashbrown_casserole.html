Prep: 15 minutes

Cook: 1 hour

Total: 1 hour 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 (32 oz) package frozen hash brown potatoes
  • 8 ounces cooked, diced ham (or to taste)
  • 2 (10.75 oz) cans condensed cream of potatoe soup
  • 1 (16 oz) container sour cream
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups grated parmesan cheese

Cheesy Ham and Hash Brown Casserole

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Lightly grease 9x13 pan.
  2. In a large bowl, mix hash browns, ham, cream of potato soup, sour cream, and Cheddar cheese.
  3. Spread evenly into prepared dish. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  4. Bake for 1 hour or until bubbly and light brown. Serve immediately.
